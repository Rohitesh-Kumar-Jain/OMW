OMW Upload How To

This page provides documentation on how to upload a wordnet to the OMW. Doing so may be unintuitive, so here is a step-by-step guide in doing so:

  1. Login to OMW by clicking on the user icon . This will direct you to a login page, where the username and password should just be admin.
  2. To upload a new wordnet, go to CILI and at the bottom of the page will be a link to the upload page, entitled "Upload a new wordnet".
